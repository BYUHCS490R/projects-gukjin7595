Box Model

What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tags starts on a new line, but Inline tags do not start on a new line. And Block tags can take up full width, but Inline tags are depends on the content.

What is the margin, border, and padding?

Margin separates the element from other elements. Border is the line that surrounds the padding and the content. Padding is the space inside the element. it is between the border and content.

What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

There are many units of measurement such as px,pt,im,cm,%,em,rem,vw and vh

When would you use a figure tag?

When I want to group media content like image, chart, etc.
