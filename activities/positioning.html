What is normal flow in HTML?

Normal flow in HTML is basic way elements placed on a page, from top to bottom in order.

What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

Float moves element to the left or right and lets text wrap around it. clear is used to stop other element.

What do absolute, fixed, and relative do?

Relative moves element from its normal position. Absolute positions an element based on its parent and Fixed positions element based on the screen and stays in place when scrolling
